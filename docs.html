Overview

The Lofita NFT Auction Platform is your gateway to the exciting world of NFT trading on the Sui blockchain. Designed to spotlight the vibrant Mystic Yeti by Lofi collection, our platform also welcomes all NFTs listed on Tradeport. With a seamless auction system powered by Tradeport’s technology, owners can list their NFTs, collectors can bid with confidence, and the highest bidder claims the prize. This whitepaper unveils our vision, the auction experience, and why this platform is a must-watch opportunity for investors.

Auction Process

Our auction system is designed to be simple, secure, and thrilling, ensuring value for NFT owners and excitement for bidders.

Listing Your NFTs

Owners can easily list their Mystic Yeti NFTs or any Tradeport-listed NFT. They choose the auction duration (like 24 hours or 7 days) and set a starting bid in SUI or LOFITA tokens. The NFT is securely locked during the auction, giving bidders confidence. Mystic Yeti NFTs get prime placement on our platform, drawing attention from collectors worldwide.

Bidding with Experience

Collectors bid by offering an amount higher than the current top bid (e.g., at least 0.1 SUI more). Bids are securely held, and live updates keep the auction exciting and transparent. Everyone can see the action unfold, making every bid a moment of anticipation.

Winning the Prize

When the auction ends, the highest bidder receives the NFT instantly. The seller gets the winning bid minus a 2.5% fee (or 1.5% with LOFITA tokens), and other bidders are refunded quickly. It’s a smooth, fair process that rewards participation and delivers results.
